1. Richard Jones Will.

In the Name of God Amen. I Richard Jones of Charles County
in the province of Maryland lying very sick and weake but of perfect
minde & memory blessed be to God for I doe hereby make this my last
will and testament in manner & form followeth.

[?] I commit my soule to God the father of our Lord Jesus Christ by whome
[?] Death & passion I hope for salvation.

I give and bequeath unto my son Richard Jones item cows[?] mare to
be under three years of age and then to enjoy the benefit of his Estate in
his own hands and to bee free from all servitude at he age of sixteen
either from his mother or any other person whatsoever.

I give and bequeath unto my Daughter Ann Jones item[?] cows more to bee
under three year old and to enjoy the Benefit of the [?] at sixteen years
old and to have the free possession of them in her own hands to be free
from all servitude either from her mother or any other person whatsoever
that is if my son or Daughter will not stay with their mother after the
age of sixteen and till they are ---- at sixteen they shall live
with their mother.

I give and bequeath to my son Richard one Iron hot two guns
six puter dishes & six silver plates [?] one bat maire with
ablaze[?] in her face aged about six years with her [?] [?] and she and
her increase to [?] for the use of the said Richard & Ann Jones well they
