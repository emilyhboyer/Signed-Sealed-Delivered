In the name of God Amen I William Allen of Charles County in the
province of maryland and being weak in body but of perfect minde memory.
prasied be God for the same doe make & ordaine this my last will &
testament I hereby working all farmer [mills?] whatsoever This [unclear] day of
January in the year of our Lord one thousand six hundred sixty nine
and first of all I bequeath my soule into the hands of almighty God who
gave it and my body unto the ground from where it came and for–
what small estate it haith please God to lend me I desire it may be–
bestowed in manner following–
