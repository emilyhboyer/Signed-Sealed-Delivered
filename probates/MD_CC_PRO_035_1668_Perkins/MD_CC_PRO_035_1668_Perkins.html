1. Robert Perkins. Will

In the name of God Amen, the thirteenth day of December in the
year of out Lord One Thousand six hundred and sixty eight I Robert Perkins
of Port Tobacco in Charles County and province of maryland planter being
sick in body but of good and heartful[?] mind & memory thanks bee to–
Almighty God and calling to remembrance the uncertains[?] Estate of this
hamsitory[?] life and that all flesh must yield unto Death when it
shall please God to call [?] make constitute Ordaine– I Declare this
my last will and Testament in manner and form I[?] allowing [?] working
and anulling by these presents all and [?] Testament and Testament
will and wills heretofore by me made and declared either by word or
writinge and this is to be taken only for my last will and Testament & more
other. And first being pertinent and for my from the Bottom of my heart–
for my sins past most humbly desireing forgiven ef[?] for the same
I give & commit my soule unto almighty God my savior and Redeemer [?]

Bartholomew Coates
