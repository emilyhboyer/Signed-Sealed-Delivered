1. Charles County
John Lumbrozo. Will

In the name of God amen, the 24th day of February 1665
I John Lumbrozo of Mangemil[?] in the province of Maryland being
in perfect[?] sound health & memory & calling to mind the
Transitory estate of all sublunary[?] things do make and dulane[?]
this to be my last will and testament in manner and form
following working[?] and anulling by these [?] all and very
tetsement on testament, will on wills therefore by me made or
declared either by word or writing and this only & no other to
be taken for my absolute last will and testament.--

[?] [?] I bequeath my Soul to the creator [?] believing
that he will in mercy look upon it and restore it to eternall
rest and my body to the earth to have desent[?] & as touching
that wordly Estate that the Lord hath blessed[?] me with all my will
desire intent and meaning is that it bee distributed as follows
First I will and desire that all my Just Debts be truly and honestly
paid and satisfied to the uttermost farthing[?]--

2 by my will and desire is that Elizabeth Lumbrozo my dearly–
beloved wife be my sole Executor of all my Estate both reall[?] and
personall and that she and her [?] Executor, administrators, that
two years after my decease she shall shall employ all my Estate
& sent to Holland or any the conditional place[?]
both reall and personall Dowery four thousand pounds of good &
well conditioned Tobacco and Cattle bee delivered to dearly beloved sister
Phiffisa[?] Lumbrozo & 2 years that the same quantity of four thousand
pounds of good & well Conditioned tobacco H[?] to be delivered-
Likewise to my aforeaid Loving sister Phiffisa[?] Lumbrozo-
what follows unitelligible[?]

3 [?] I will & bequeath my dearly beloved friend Edward Richardson 50
acres of Land lying in mengeme[?] [?] called Lumbrozo's discovery-
adjoining to Richard Touds[?] land on the on side and Roger Dickinson's on
the other side.

4 If my wife should not acknowledge herself to be my executor

and that she will not prove or take notice of this my will then my desire is that
all my plantation and all the Land appertaining to it shall bee sold & [?]
only reserving her things during her life and the tobacco that it is sold for
to be sent to my sister Phiffisa[?] Lumbrozo.

5 I do desire and request Mr. Henry Adams of Port [?] and Mr. Lukr
Gardiner of St. Clements Bay both in the province of Maryland and Edward Richardson of London merchant to bee the overseers of this my Last
Will & Testament and to look that all things herein contained bee as
exactly performed as with all profitable[?] Convenience my be

6 by This is my last and absolute Will & Testament as [?] my hand &
seal the day and year above written after these words on the other side
interlined[?] between the 23 and 24th ;ome [?] [?] the 25th and 26th
line [?] & that she her [?] Executord Administrators or [?] shall enjoy

his
Edmont Lendsey
