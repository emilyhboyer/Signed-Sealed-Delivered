I Nicholas Emanson of Charles County in the province of Maryland being sick and
weak in body but of good and perfect memory thank be to god do make this my
last will and testament in manner and form following that is to say
first I bequeath my soul and [?] into the hand of the almighty God my heavenly father
by u[?] home of his [?] and only grace I trust to be saved and n[?] unto eternal
trust[?] though the Death of my savior and redeemer Jesus Chrust in whose precious[?]
blood I sell the whole and only hope of my salvation my weathered[?] body in hope
of a joyful resurrection I commit to the earth to be with such charge
and in such place as my Executor hereafter named shall think till[?] and
touching[?] the distribution of my mortall goods I disperse of the same a followith

  1. First I will that all such Debts as I owe shall be truly paid.
  2. Secondly I give unto my Daughter [unclear] one thousand two hundred pounds of Tobacco to be [paid?] to her or her heirs by my Executor hereafter named.
  3. All of the rest of my Estate reall and pesonal lands goods cattles and chattels Whatsoever I give and bequeath unto my loving wife Elizabeth and my Daughter Mary Emanson to be equally divided between them and my Daughter Marys share to be delivered unto her when she comes to be [sixteen?] years of age
  4.
